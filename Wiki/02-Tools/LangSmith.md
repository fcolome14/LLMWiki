# LangSmith

Tracing/observability platform for LLM apps. Captures full traces of graph runs — every node, tool call, and intermediate state.

## Why we use it
- Debugging multi-agent runs (which node made the wrong call, and why)
- Building eval datasets from real traces — feeds into [[Agent Evaluation]]
- Useful for compliance audit trails — see [[KYC Automation]]
